## Business Plan Structure

### 1. Executive Summary (1 page max)
- Business name, location, founding date
- Mission statement (1 sentence)
- What problem does the Zumba Fitness Studio solve?
- Target customer in one line
- Revenue model summary
- Funding ask (if applicable)

### 2. Business Description
A Zumba Fitness Studio provides essential zumba and studio solutions to customers in the Wellness sector.

### 3. Market Analysis
- **Target Market:** Local businesses and consumers needing zumba services
- **Market Size:** Research TAM → SAM → SOM for local/regional market
- **Customer Segments:** Individual customers (primary), Business clients (secondary)
- **Market Trends:** Digital adoption, quality demand, local preference

### 4. Competitive Analysis
| Competitor Type | Examples | Your Differentiation |
|----------------|----------|----------------------|
| Direct | Other local zumba fitness studio providers | List your edge |
| Indirect | DIY alternatives | List your edge |
| Substitute | Online alternatives | List your edge |

### 5. Products & Services
- Core Zumba Fitness Studio services
- Premium packages
- Add-on services

See [`marketing.md`](./marketing.md) for full details.

- Pricing strategy: Market-rate with quality differentiation

See [`operations.md`](./operations.md) for full details.

- Key processes: Customer intake, zumba delivery, quality check, billing

## Key Success Factors
- Quality of zumba
- Customer service responsiveness
- Consistent delivery
- Strong local reputation

## Risks & Mitigation
- Staff turnover → fix: training + retention bonuses
- Competition → fix: specialisation
- Slow season → fix: diversify revenue streams
