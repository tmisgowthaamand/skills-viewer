## Business Overview

## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Services |
| **Revenue Model** | Per-kg or per-garment, subscriptions |
| **Key Costs** | Machines, detergent, rent, staff |
| **Typical Team Size** | 2–10 |
| **Licenses Needed** | FSSAI (if hotel linen), GST |
| **Primary Tools** | Laundry management app, WhatsApp, Google Sheets |
