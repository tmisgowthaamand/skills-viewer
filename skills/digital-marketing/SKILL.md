## Business Overview

A digital marketing agency delivering SEO, paid ads (Google/Meta), social media management, content creation, email marketing, and performance analytics for business clients.

## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Professional Services / Agency |
| **Revenue Model** | Monthly retainer, project fees, performance-based commissions |
| **Key Costs** | Staff salaries, ad platform tools, software subscriptions |
| **Typical Team Size** | 3–30 people |
| **Licenses Needed** | GST registration, Company/LLP registration |
| **Primary Tools** | Google Ads, Meta Ads, SEMrush, HubSpot, Canva, GA4, Notion |

## Quick Start Checklist

- [ ] Register company (LLP or Pvt Ltd)
- [ ] Get GST registration
- [ ] Set up Google Partner and Meta Business Partner accounts
- [ ] Build portfolio website with case studies
- [ ] Define service packages with clear pricing
- [ ] Set up project management tool (Notion/Asana)
