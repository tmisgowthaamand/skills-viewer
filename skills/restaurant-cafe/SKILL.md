## Business Overview

A dine-in restaurant and cafe providing food, beverages, and hospitality experiences to walk-in customers, delivery platforms, and private events.

## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Food & Beverage / Hospitality |
| **Revenue Model** | Dine-in covers, takeaway, delivery (Zomato/Swiggy), catering |
| **Key Costs** | Raw materials (F&B cost 28–35%), rent, staff, utilities |
| **Typical Team Size** | 5–30 people |
| **Licenses Needed** | FSSAI, GST, Shop & Establishment, Municipal trade licence, Fire NOC, Eating House licence |
| **Primary Tools** | Petpooja/Posist POS, Zomato/Swiggy Partner, WhatsApp Business, Tally |

## Quick Start Checklist

- [ ] Secure location + get municipal/eating house licence
- [ ] Apply for FSSAI Food Business Operator licence
- [ ] Get GST registration
- [ ] Install POS (Petpooja or Posist)
- [ ] Onboard Zomato and Swiggy
- [ ] Set up Google My Business
- [ ] Standardize menu with recipe cards
