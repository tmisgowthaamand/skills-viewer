## Core SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)

**SOP 1: Customer Inquiry to Booking**
1. Receive inquiry via WhatsApp/phone
2. Understand requirements
3. Send quote within 2 hours
4. Confirm booking with advance payment
5. Add to calendar

**SOP 2: Service Delivery**
1. Prepare materials/team in advance
2. Arrive/start on time
3. Deliver service per standard
4. Quality check before completion
5. Collect payment
6. Request review

## Daily / Weekly / Monthly Checklists

### Daily Opening Checklist
- [ ] Check WhatsApp and email for new inquiries
- [ ] Confirm today's bookings/schedule
- [ ] Prepare materials for today's youtube work
- [ ] Follow up on pending quotes

### Weekly Review Checklist
- [ ] Review revenue vs target
- [ ] Review customer complaints / feedback
- [ ] Check inventory / stock levels
- [ ] Review staff attendance and performance
- [ ] Pay urgent vendor invoices
- [ ] Update social media

### Monthly Management Checklist
- [ ] P&L review
- [ ] Team performance reviews
- [ ] Vendor contract reviews
- [ ] Update SOPs if processes changed
- [ ] Review KPIs vs targets
- [ ] File GST / compliance obligations
- [ ] Plan next month's goals

## HR Policies (Starter Templates)

### Leave Policy
- Casual Leave: 12 days/year
- Sick Leave: 7 days/year
- Public Holidays: As per state govt calendar
- 12 casual + 7 sick leaves as per standard

### Salary Structure Template
```
Basic:          40% of CTC
HRA:            20% of Basic
Allowances:     Balance
PF (Employer):  12% of Basic
ESI (Employer): 3.25% of Gross (if applicable)
```

### Performance Review Cadence
- Monthly 1:1 check-ins (15 min)
- Quarterly KPI review
- Annual appraisal with increment decision

## Key Operational KPIs

| KPI | Target |
|-----|--------|
| Service completion on-time % | >95% |
| Customer complaint rate | <2% |
| Monthly revenue vs target | Track weekly |
| Staff utilisation | >80% |

## Common Operational Mistakes for YouTube Content Business
- No written SOPs → inconsistent youtube quality
- No advance payment → no-shows
- Not tracking inventory → running out of supplies
- Ignoring negative reviews
