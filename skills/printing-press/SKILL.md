## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Manufacturing/Services |
| **Revenue Model** | Per-job printing, bulk contracts |
| **Key Costs** | Machines, materials, rent, staff |
| **Typical Team Size** | 3–20 |
| **Licenses Needed** | GST, Pollution NOC (offset) |
| **Primary Tools** | Adobe InDesign, RIP software, Tally, IndiaMART |
