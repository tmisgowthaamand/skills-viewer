# 🏪 Supermarket — Business Plan & Strategy

## When to Use This File
- User asks to write a business plan for a Supermarket
- User wants an investor pitch / startup plan
- Keywords: `business plan`, `strategy`, `supermarket`, `services`

---

## Business Plan Structure

### 1. Executive Summary (1 page max)
- Business name, location, founding date
- Mission statement (1 sentence)
- What problem does the Supermarket solve?
- Target customer in one line
- Revenue model summary
- Funding ask (if applicable)

### 2. Business Description
A Supermarket provides essential supermarket and services solutions to customers in the Retail sector.

### 3. Market Analysis
- **Target Market:** Local businesses and consumers needing supermarket services
- **Market Size:** Research TAM → SAM → SOM for local/regional market
- **Customer Segments:** Individual customers (primary), Business clients (secondary)
- **Market Trends:** Digital adoption, quality demand, local preference

### 4. Competitive Analysis
| Competitor Type | Examples | Your Differentiation |
|----------------|----------|----------------------|
| Direct | Other local supermarket providers | List your edge |
| Indirect | DIY alternatives | List your edge |
| Substitute | Online alternatives | List your edge |

### 5. Products & Services
- Core Supermarket services
- Premium packages
- Add-on services

### 6. Marketing & Sales Strategy
See [`marketing.md`](./marketing.md) for full details.
- Primary channels: Google My Business, WhatsApp, Instagram
- Pricing strategy: Market-rate with quality differentiation
- Sales process: Inquiry → Quote → Confirmation → Delivery → Follow-up

### 7. Operations Plan
See [`operations.md`](./operations.md) for full details.
- Location: Retail premises
- Key processes: Customer intake, supermarket delivery, quality check, billing

### 8. Management & Team
- Key roles needed: Owner, supermarket specialist, customer service, admin
- Founder background section
- Advisory board (if applicable)

### 9. Financial Projections (3 Years)
See [`finance.md`](./finance.md) for templates.

|------|---------------|-----------------|------------|
| Y1   | ₹X            | ₹X              | ₹X         |
| Y2   | ₹X (+growth%) | ₹X              | ₹X         |
| Y3   | ₹X (+growth%) | ₹X              | ₹X         |

### 10. Funding Requirements
- Amount needed: ₹3–15 lakh for first year
- Use of funds: Equipment/setup (50%), working capital (30%), marketing (20%)
- Repayment or equity plan

---

## Key Success Factors
- Quality of supermarket
- Customer service responsiveness
- Consistent delivery
- Strong local reputation

## Risks & Mitigation
- Staff turnover → fix: training + retention bonuses
- Competition → fix: specialisation
- Slow season → fix: diversify revenue streams
