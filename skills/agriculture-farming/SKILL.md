## Business Overview

Crop cultivation, livestock, and agri-produce distribution.

## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Agriculture |
| **Revenue Model** | Crop/livestock sales, B2B supply |
| **Key Costs** | Seeds, fertiliser, labour, equipment |
| **Typical Team Size** | 5–50 |
| **Licenses Needed** | Kisan registration, APEDA (export) |
| **Primary Tools** | AgroStar, Cropin, soil testing apps |
