## Business Overview

A bakery producing and selling bread, cakes, pastries, cookies, and baked goods through a retail store, online orders, custom cake business, and wholesale to cafes/hotels.

## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Food & Beverage / Retail |
| **Revenue Model** | Walk-in retail, online orders, wholesale, custom orders (cakes) |
| **Key Costs** | Raw materials (flour, butter, sugar), labour, equipment, rent |
| **Typical Team Size** | 2–15 people |
| **Licenses Needed** | FSSAI licence, GST, Shop & Establishment Act, Fire NOC |
| **Primary Tools** | Petpooja POS, WhatsApp Business, Instagram, Tally |

## Quick Start Checklist

- [ ] Get FSSAI Food Business Operator licence
- [ ] Register GST (if turnover >₹20L)
- [ ] Set up production schedule and recipe cards
- [ ] Install POS system (Petpooja)
- [ ] Open WhatsApp Business account for orders
- [ ] Set up Instagram business account
