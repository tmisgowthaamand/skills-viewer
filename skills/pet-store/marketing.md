## When to Use This File
- User wants a marketing plan for a Pet Store & Pet Care

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Who** | Local customer needing pet services |
| **Age Range** | 25–50 |
| **Location** | Local area (within 5 km) |
| **Pain Points** | Can't find reliable, quality pet provider |
| **Why They Buy** | Quality, reliability, price, convenience |
| **Where They Are** | Google Maps, WhatsApp referrals, Instagram |

---

## Channel Strategy

### 🥇 Primary Channels (highest ROI for Pet Store & Pet Care)
1. **Google My Business** — Critical for local discovery
2. **WhatsApp Business** — Primary inquiry and order channel
3. **Instagram** — Visual portfolio and trust building

### 🥈 Secondary Channels
- Local referral network
- Festive season promotions
- Tie-ups with complementary businesses

### 🥉 Channels to Avoid (low ROI for this business)
- Mass print advertising (low ROI)
- Yellow pages

## Paid Advertising Guide

### Google Ads
- Campaign type: Local search ads
- Target keywords: 'pet near me', 'pet store & pet care [city]', 'best pet [area]'
- Budget: Start ₹300–500/day
- Goal: Phone call or WhatsApp inquiry

### Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)
- Audience: Local residents interested in pet, age 25–50
- Ad format: Photo portfolio + customer review ads
- Budget: Start ₹200–300/day
- Goal: Inquiry / DM

## Customer Retention Playbook
- Follow up 30 days after service
- Birthday / anniversary reminder
- Loyalty reward after 5 purchases

## Sales Process (Inquiry → Conversion)
Inquiry → Quote sent within 2 hours → Follow up within 24 hours → Booking confirmation → Service delivery → Review request
