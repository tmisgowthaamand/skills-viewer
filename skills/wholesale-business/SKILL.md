## Business Overview

B2B bulk distribution and trading.

## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Trade/Distribution |
| **Revenue Model** | Sales margin on bulk goods |
| **Key Costs** | Inventory, warehouse, transport, credit |
| **Typical Team Size** | 5–30 |
| **Licenses Needed** | GST, Shop & Est. |
| **Primary Tools** | Tally, Marg ERP, IndiaMART, WhatsApp Business |

## Quick Start Checklist

- [ ] Register business (GST + local licences)
- [ ] Set up Tally as primary tool
- [ ] Create service/product menu with pricing
- [ ] Open Google My Business
- [ ] Set up WhatsApp Business
- [ ] Define 3 core SOPs
