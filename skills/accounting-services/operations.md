## 🔄 Client Onboarding Workflow

**Step 1: Lead Capture** → Website form / WhatsApp / Phone call / Referral

**Step 2: Consultation Call** → Understand business type, turnover, filing needs → Assign tier

**Step 3: Document Collection** → KYC documents (PAN, Aadhaar, GST certificate, bank details)

**Step 4: Setup & Configuration** → Create client in Tally/Zoho Books → Set up filing calendar → Assign account manager

**Step 5: Active Service** → Monthly bookkeeping → Periodic tax filing → Quarterly reviews

## Core SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)

### SOP 1: New Client Onboarding (Timeline: 3 days)

| Step | Action | Owner | SLA |
|------|--------|-------|-----|
| 1 | Receive inquiry & qualify lead | Sales / Admin | Within 2 hours |
| 2 | Schedule consultation call | Account Manager | Same day |
| 3 | Assess needs → recommend tier & pricing | Account Manager | During call |
| 4 | Send proposal + engagement letter | Account Manager | Within 24 hours |
| 5 | Collect signed agreement + documents | Admin | Within 48 hours |
| 6 | Create client in system (Tally/Zoho CRM) | Accountant | Within 24 hours |
| 7 | Assign account manager + set filing calendar | Operations Head | Same day |
| 8 | Welcome email with compliance calendar | Admin | Immediately |

### SOP 2: GST Return Filing

| Step | Action | Owner | SLA |
|------|--------|-------|-----|
| 1 | Collect client's sales & purchase data | Accountant | By 5th of month |
| 2 | Reconcile with bank statements | Accountant | By 7th |
| 3 | Prepare GSTR-1 (outward supplies) | GST Executive | By 10th |
| 4 | Client review & approval | Account Manager | By 11th |
| 5 | File GSTR-1 on portal | GST Executive | By 11th/13th |
| 6 | Prepare GSTR-3B (summary + tax payment) | GST Executive | By 18th |
| 7 | Client approval for tax payment | Account Manager | By 19th |
| 8 | File GSTR-3B + make tax payment | GST Executive | By 20th |

### SOP 3: ITR Filing

| Step | Action | Owner | SLA |
|------|--------|-------|-----|
| 1 | Collect Form 16 / P&L / Balance Sheet | Accountant | April–May |
| 2 | Compute taxable income | Tax Executive | Within 5 days |
| 3 | Identify deductions (80C, 80D, HRA etc.) | Tax Executive | Same time |
| 4 | Prepare computation sheet | Tax Executive | Within 3 days |
| 5 | Client review & confirmation | Account Manager | Within 48 hours |
| 6 | File ITR on e-filing portal | Tax Executive | Within 24 hours |
| 7 | Send acknowledgment (ITR-V) to client | Admin | Same day |

## Daily / Weekly / Monthly Checklists

### Daily Checklist
- [ ] Check all client communication (WhatsApp, email, CRM)
- [ ] Follow up on pending document requests
- [ ] Process any urgent filings (TDS deposits by 7th)
- [ ] Update task management board (Asana/Trello)
- [ ] Respond to client queries within 2 hours

### Weekly Review
- [ ] Review week's completed vs pending filings
- [ ] Check upcoming compliance deadlines (next 2 weeks)
- [ ] Team sync meeting (30 min)
- [ ] Review client feedback / satisfaction
- [ ] Update social media (2 posts minimum)
- [ ] Review lead pipeline and follow-ups

### Monthly Management Review
- [ ] P&L review vs budget
- [ ] Client count: new additions vs churned
- [ ] Team performance review (filing accuracy, SLA adherence)
- [ ] Vendor/subscription cost review
- [ ] Update SOPs if any process changed
- [ ] Review all compliance obligations met
- [ ] Plan next month's marketing & operations goals

## ⚠️ Common Operational Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| No written SOPs | Inconsistent service quality | Document all processes |
| Missing filing deadlines | Penalties for clients | Automated calendar alerts |
| No document backup | Risk of data loss | Cloud storage + weekly backups |
| Over-dependence on founder | Bottleneck, burnout | Delegate + train team |
| Not tracking KPIs | Can't measure growth | Dashboard with weekly reviews |
| Ignoring client feedback | High churn rate | Quarterly NPS surveys |
| No advance payment policy | Cash flow problems | 50% advance, rest after filing |
