## Business Plan Structure

### 1. Executive Summary

**Mission:** To democratize professional accounting services in India — making tax compliance, bookkeeping, and financial advisory accessible and affordable for every segment from freelancers to enterprises.

**Problem Solved:** India has 63M+ MSMEs and millions of individuals struggling with complex GST, income tax, and compliance requirements. Most lack access to affordable, reliable accounting support.

**Solution:** A tiered accounting services platform offering plans from ₹999/year (freelancers) to custom enterprise pricing, combining technology (cloud accounting) with expert CA support.

---

### 2. Customer Segmentation — 5 Tiers

![Business Tiers Pyramid](/skills/accounting-services/images/business-tiers-pyramid.png)

#### 🟢 Tier 1: Micro / Individual (Freelancers, Gig Workers, Students)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Target** | Freelancers, tuition teachers, Uber/Swiggy drivers, content creators, students |
| **Annual Income** | Below ₹10 Lakh |
| **Key Needs** | ITR filing, basic bookkeeping, TDS refund, Section 80C planning |
| **Price Range** | ₹999 – ₹4,999/year |
| **GST Required?** | No (below ₹20L threshold) |
| **Popular Tools** | ClearTax, Zoho Invoice, Google Sheets |
| **Tax Tip** | Under Section 44ADA, declare 50% of gross receipts as income (up to ₹75L) |

#### 🔵 Tier 2: Small Business (Shopkeepers, Kirana, Local Services)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Target** | Retail shops, restaurants, salons, local traders, service providers |
| **Annual Turnover** | ₹10 Lakh – ₹1 Crore |
| **Key Needs** | GST filing, billing/invoicing, stock tracking, bank reconciliation |
| **Price Range** | ₹4,999 – ₹14,999/year |
| **GST Required?** | Yes (if turnover > ₹40L for goods / ₹20L for services) |
| **Popular Tools** | TallyPrime, Vyapar, KhataBook |
| **Tax Tip** | Use GST Composition Scheme — 1% tax, quarterly filing, for turnover up to ₹1.5 Cr |

#### 🟡 Tier 3: Mid-Level Business (Growing SMEs)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Target** | Manufacturing units, IT services, e-commerce sellers, agencies |
| **Annual Turnover** | ₹1 Crore – ₹25 Crore |
| **Key Needs** | Full accounting, TDS/TCS, payroll, PF/ESI, quarterly GST, audit support |
| **Price Range** | ₹14,999 – ₹49,999/year |
| **GST Required?** | Yes, with quarterly/monthly filing |
| **Popular Tools** | Zoho Books, QuickBooks, Tally ERP |
| **Tax Tip** | MSME benefits — 18% corporate tax rate, priority lending, CGTMSE loans |

#### 🟠 Tier 4: Large / High-Growth (Established Companies)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Target** | Private limited companies, LLPs with multiple branches |
| **Annual Turnover** | ₹25 Crore – ₹500 Crore |
| **Key Needs** | Statutory audit, virtual CFO, transfer pricing, MIS reports, advanced tax planning |
| **Price Range** | ₹49,999 – ₹2,99,999/year |
| **GST Required?** | Yes, monthly filing + e-invoicing mandatory |
| **Popular Tools** | SAP, Oracle ERP, custom solutions |
| **Tax Tip** | Transfer pricing documentation mandatory for related-party transactions |

#### 🔴 Tier 5: Enterprise / MNC (Conglomerates)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Target** | Multinational companies, listed corporations, government PSUs |
| **Annual Turnover** | ₹500 Crore+ |
| **Key Needs** | Full compliance suite, forensic audit, international taxation, IFRS, board-level reporting |
| **Price Range** | Custom pricing (₹5L – ₹50L+/year) |
| **GST Required?** | Yes, complex multi-state, multi-entity compliance |
| **Popular Tools** | SAP S/4HANA, Oracle Cloud, Workday |
| **Tax Tip** | Advance ruling applications can save significant tax on complex transactions |

## Key Success Factors

- ✅ Quality and accuracy of tax filing & compliance
- ✅ Responsive customer service (< 2 hour response time)
- ✅ Technology-first approach with personal CA support
- ✅ Strong local reputation and Google reviews
- ✅ Tiered pricing making services accessible to all segments

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Staff turnover | Training programs + retention bonuses + career growth |
| Competition from free tools | Value-added advisory services + personal support |
| Regulatory changes | Continuous education + automated compliance updates |
| Slow season (Nov–Jan) | Diversify with advisory, training courses, annual compliance |
| Client payment delays | Advance payment policy + automated reminders |
