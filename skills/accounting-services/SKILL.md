## Business Overview

A professional accounting firm providing bookkeeping, GST filing, tax planning, payroll, audit support, and financial advisory services to SMEs and individuals.

## Industry at a Glance

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Type** | Professional Services / B2B |
| **Revenue Model** | Monthly retainer, per-project fees, annual contracts |
| **Key Costs** | Staff salaries, software licences, office rent |
| **Typical Team Size** | 2–20 people |
| **Licenses Needed** | CA certificate (ICAI), GST registration, Professional Tax |
| **Primary Tools** | Tally Prime, QuickBooks, Zoho Books, MS Excel |

## Quick Start Checklist

- [ ] Register CA firm with ICAI
- [ ] Get GST registration
- [ ] Set up Tally/QuickBooks for client billing
- [ ] Create standard chart of accounts templates
- [ ] Build client onboarding packet (NDA + engagement letter)
- [ ] Define service menu with pricing
