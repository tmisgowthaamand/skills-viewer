## 📣 Paid Advertising Guide

### Google Ads
- **Campaign Type:** Local search ads + search campaigns
- **Target Keywords:** 'CA near me', 'GST filing service [city]', 'income tax filing online'
- **Budget:** Start ₹500–₹1,000/day
- **Goal:** Phone calls, WhatsApp inquiry, form fills
- **Expected CPC:** ₹15–₹40 for accounting keywords

### Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)
- **Audience:** Business owners, 25–55, interested in finance & entrepreneurship
- **Ad Format:** Carousel (service showcase) + Video (client testimonials)
- **Budget:** Start ₹300–₹500/day
- **Goal:** Lead forms, DMs, website visits

### LinkedIn Ads
- **Audience:** CFOs, founders, HR heads, finance managers
- **Ad Format:** Sponsored content + InMail
- **Budget:** Start ₹500–₹1,000/day
- **Goal:** B2B leads for mid-level & enterprise clients
