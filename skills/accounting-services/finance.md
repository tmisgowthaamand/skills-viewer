## 🇮🇳 Income Tax Structure — FY 2025-26

### New Tax Regime (Default)

| Income Slab | Tax Rate |
|-------------|----------|
| Up to ₹4,00,000 | **Nil** |
| ₹4,00,001 – ₹8,00,000 | **5%** |
| ₹8,00,001 – ₹12,00,000 | **10%** |
| ₹12,00,001 – ₹16,00,000 | **15%** |
| ₹16,00,001 – ₹20,00,000 | **20%** |
| ₹20,00,001 – ₹24,00,000 | **25%** |
| Above ₹24,00,000 | **30%** |

> **Rebate u/s 87A**: Income up to ₹12,00,000 → **ZERO TAX** under new regime!

### Corporate Tax Rates

| Category | Tax Rate |
|----------|----------|
| MSMEs (turnover < ₹50 Cr) | **18%** (reduced from 22%) |
| New companies (started after Apr 2025) | **15%** |
| Other domestic companies | **22%** (without exemptions) |
| Startups — LTCG on equity investments | **8%** |

## GST Structure (Effective September 2025)

### GST Rate Slabs (Simplified)

| Slab | Rate | Items Covered |
|------|------|---------------|
| **Essential** | **0%** | Basic food grains, fresh produce, healthcare |
| **Lower** | **5%** | Packaged food, footwear (<₹1000), economy travel |
| **Standard** | **18%** | Most services, electronics, processed food, restaurants |
| **Luxury/Sin** | **28%** | Luxury cars, tobacco, aerated drinks |

### GST Registration Thresholds (2025-26)

| Category | Threshold |
|----------|-----------|
| Goods suppliers | ₹40 Lakh |
| Service providers | ₹20 Lakh |
| Special category states (NE & hill) | ₹10 Lakh |
| Inter-state suppliers | Mandatory (any turnover) |
| E-commerce sellers | Mandatory (any turnover) |

### GST Filing Calendar

| Return | Frequency | Due Date | Who Files |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| **GSTR-1** | Monthly/Quarterly | 11th/13th of next month | All registered dealers |
| **GSTR-3B** | Monthly/Quarterly | 20th of next month | All registered dealers |
| **GSTR-9** | Annual | 31st December | Turnover > ₹2 Cr |
| **GSTR-9C** | Annual (Audit) | 31st December | Turnover > ₹5 Cr |
| **CMP-08** | Quarterly | 18th of next month | Composition scheme dealers |

## TDS Compliance — Key Sections

| Section | Payment Type | TDS Rate | Threshold |
|---------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| **192** | Salary | As per income tax slab | As per tax slab |
| **194A** | Interest (non-bank) | 10% | ₹5,000/year |
| **194C** | Contractor payments | 1% (Individual) / 2% (Others) | ₹30,000 (single) / ₹1L (aggregate) |

## 🏛️ Government Schemes — Detailed

### 💰 Financial Support Schemes

| Scheme | Benefits | Eligible For | Amount |
|--------|----------|-------------|--------|
| **PMMY (Mudra Yojana)** | Low-interest business loans | Non-corporate, non-farm MSMEs | Up to ₹10 Lakh |
| ↳ Shishu | For starting businesses | New entrepreneurs | Up to ₹50,000 |
| ↳ Kishor | For growing businesses | Growing MSMEs | ₹50,001 – ₹5 Lakh |
| ↳ Tarun | For established businesses | Established MSMEs | ₹5,00,001 – ₹10 Lakh |
| **CGTMSE** | Collateral-free loans | Micro & Small enterprises | Up to ₹5 Crore |
| **PMEGP** | Credit-linked subsidy | New micro businesses | Up to ₹25L (manufacturing) / ₹10L (service) |
| **Startup India Seed Fund** | Early-stage funding | DPIIT-registered startups | From ₹2,500 Cr corpus |
| **Stand-Up India** | Women & SC/ST entrepreneurship | Women/SC/ST entrepreneurs | ₹10L – ₹1 Cr |
| **CLCSS** | 15% capital subsidy for tech upgrade | MSMEs | On investment up to ₹1 Cr |

### 🎯 Tax Benefits for MSMEs & Startups (2025-26)

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Lower Corporate Tax | 18% for MSMEs (turnover < ₹50 Cr) |
| Startup Tax Holiday | 5-year tax holiday (extended to March 2030) |
| New Company Rate | 15% for companies starting from April 2025 |
| LTCG for Startups | Reduced to 8% on equity investments |
| Presumptive Taxation | 8%/6% profit rate for turnover up to ₹2 Cr |
| Quarterly GST | For turnover under ₹5 Cr |
| GST Exemption | Raised from ₹20L to ₹40L |

### 🆕 Key Policy Changes (2025-26)

| Policy | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| GST Rate Simplification | Reduced from 4 slabs to 2 (5% & 18%) — effective Sep 2025 |
| Faster GST Registration | Processing time reduced to 3 days |
| E-invoicing Mandate | Mandatory for turnover ≥ ₹10 Cr |
| Small Companies Definition | Capital up to ₹10 Cr & turnover up to ₹100 Cr |
| Digital Data Protection Act | Privacy compliance required from 2026 |
| Free Accounting Software | Government plans embedded free tools for micro-enterprises |
